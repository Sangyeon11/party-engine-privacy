# Privacy Policy — Nori (PartyEngine)

_Last updated: 2026-04-28_

Nori (the "App") is a local party-game app published by **sangyeon11** (the "Developer").
This document explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

If you have any questions, contact: **a01025110935@gmail.com**

---

## English

### 1. Data we do **not** collect

Nori is designed to work without an account. We do **not** collect:

- Your name, phone number, email, or any directly identifying personal data
- Your contacts, photos, location, microphone, or camera
- Your friends list or any social-graph data
- Any messages typed inside the app (player names, custom penalties) — these stay on your device

### 2. Data stored on your device

The following are saved only in local storage on your phone, and never sent to a server we control:

- Selected mode, language, theme, drinks-mode toggle, sound toggle
- Player names entered for the current session
- Custom penalty list you created
- Your current and recent played games (for recommendations)
- Tutorial completion flags
- Whether you have a Nori Pro entitlement (cached from the store)

You can delete all of the above at any time by uninstalling the App.

### 3. Third-party services

Nori uses the following third-party services. Their own privacy policies apply.

| Service | Purpose | Data shared |
|---|---|---|
| **Sentry** | Crash reporting and anonymous diagnostic logs | Device model, OS version, app version, anonymous error stack. No personal identifiers. Disabled if `EXPO_PUBLIC_SENTRY_DSN` is unset for that build. |
| **Google AdMob** | Show ads in the result screen for non-Pro users | Mobile advertising ID (resettable from your phone settings), coarse device info. Personalized ads are **disabled** by default — only non-personalized ads are requested. See [Google AdMob policy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites). |
| **App Store / Google Play / RevenueCat** | Process in-app purchases (Nori Pro, season packs) | Anonymous purchase token. We do **not** receive your name, email, or payment details. Refunds follow each store's own policy. |
| **Expo Updates** | Deliver minor over-the-air JavaScript updates | Anonymous request to fetch the update bundle, including app version and runtime version. |

### 4. Children

Nori contains content that may not be suitable for users under the appropriate age rating
in your country (commonly 17+). We do not knowingly market to or collect data from
children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, contact us and we will
investigate.

### 5. Your rights

Because Nori does not store personal data on a server, there is no account to delete.
You can:

- **Erase all on-device data**: uninstall the App.
- **Reset your advertising ID**: Android Settings → Privacy → Ads → Reset advertising ID.
- **Restore purchases**: Open the App → Help → Restore purchases.
- **Request more info**: email **a01025110935@gmail.com**.

### 6. Changes

If this policy changes materially, we will update the "Last updated" date and surface
the change inside the App. Continued use of the App after a change constitutes acceptance.
